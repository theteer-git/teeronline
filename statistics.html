Understanding Teer Frequency Analysis

Frequency analysis tracks how often each digit (0-9) appears as the house digit (first digit) or ending digit (second digit) in Teer results. This data helps enthusiasts understand which digits are currently "hot" (appearing frequently) or "cold" (appearing rarely). However, remember that Teer is a game of chance based on archery outcomes - past frequency does not guarantee future results.
