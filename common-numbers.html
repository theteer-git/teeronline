How Teer House, Ending and Direct Numbers Are Shown

Teer House Number

The house number refers to the first digit of a two-digit Teer result. For example, in 45, the house number is 4.

Teer Ending Number

The ending number refers to the last digit of a two-digit Teer result. For example, in 45, the ending number is 5.

Teer Direct Number

The direct number is the full two-digit number, such as 45, 79 or 08. This page shows direct common numbers using calculated digit references and historical patterns.
