Teer Common Numbers - Mathematical Approach

Shillong Teer Common Numbers and Khanapara Teer Common Numbers are derived using complement logic. Take all four digits from today's FR and SR, add complements (0↔5,1↔6,2↔7,3↔8,4↔9), then find missing digits from 0-9. These missing digits are tomorrow's common digits. If any appears as house (tens) or ending (units) in next day's FR/SR, it's a HIT.
