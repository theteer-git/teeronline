Khanapara Teer FR and SR Timings

The declared First Round time is 4:25 PM, followed by the Second Round at 5:00 PM, Indian Standard Time. Actual publication may vary by a few minutes. Before relying on a number, confirm the date shown on the result card and check whether the round is marked pending, partial or completed.

The regular Khanapara Teer day game is generally not scheduled on Sunday. On a non-playing day, visitors should rely on the displayed date and status rather than assuming that the most recently visible historical number belongs to the current day.

How to Read the Khanapara Teer Result Status

First Round (FR)

FR means First Round. When a valid two-digit First Round result is available, it replaces the XX placeholder in the FR box. Until then, the round remains pending.

Second Round (SR)

SR means Second Round. It is displayed independently from FR so the page can show an available First Round result without presenting an unconfirmed Second Round value.

Pending, partial and completed

Pending means no valid number has been recorded for the relevant round. Partial means one round is available while the other is still awaited. Completed means both FR and SR have been recorded for the displayed date.
