⚡ Formula 2: Combined Groups & Points Strategy

When you receive two different predictions (e.g., Group 17 OR Point 13), the intersecting numbers are your strongest hits.

Scenario: Prediction 1 says Group 17 (8 numbers), Prediction 2 says 13 points (6 numbers)

Intersection: 76 or 67 are common in both predictions → These should be your hit numbers for the day!

The remaining 10+ numbers are normal probability numbers.
