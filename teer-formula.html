📐 Understanding Digit Values

In Teer mathematics, each digit from 0-4 has a corresponding value. This is the foundation of all Teer calculations.

0 ➜ 5
1 ➜ 6
2 ➜ 7
3 ➜ 8
4 ➜ 9

Using this concept, any number can be expanded. For example, 75 expands to 20 or 02 and so on.
