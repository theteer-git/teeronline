Shillong Teer FR and SR schedule

The declared First Round time is 4:15 PM and the declared Second Round time is 5:15 PM IST. Actual publication can occur slightly before or after the declared time. The regular Shillong Teer day game is generally not scheduled on Sunday.

How to read the live result status

FR means First Round and SR means Second Round. Results are shown as two-digit numbers from 00 to 99. Pending means the relevant round has not yet been recorded for the displayed date; Partial means one round is available; Completed means both rounds are available.

Previous Shillong Teer results

The compact history panel shows recent completed records. For a wider date-wise search, open the Shillong Teer previous-results archive. The archive provides older FR and SR records without mixing them with today’s live status.

Shillong Teer common numbers and statistics

The common-number and statistics panels summarize historical data through house and ending digits, repeated numbers, hot and cold numbers, missing periods, recent trends and past performance. They are reference material only and are not official results or guaranteed predictions.

Result accuracy and corrections

TeerOnline keeps a round pending until a valid result is available rather than carrying an older number forward as today’s result. When a historical correction is required, it is applied to the corresponding date. Visitors may report a suspected error through the contact page.

Frequently Asked Questions about Shillong Teer

What time is the Shillong Teer result published?
