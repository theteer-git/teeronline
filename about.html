📖 Our Story

Teer Results Archive was founded with a simple mission: to provide accurate, timely, and reliable Teer results for enthusiasts across Meghalaya. What started as a small personal project has grown into a comprehensive resource for Shillong, Khanapara, Juwai, and Night Teer results.

We understand the importance of having access to authentic Teer data. Our platform is built on the principles of transparency, accuracy, and informational integrity. We do not promote gambling or betting; instead, we serve as a historical archive and reference point for those interested in this traditional Meghalaya archery game.
